Paint Plus is a modified version of Paint for Windows XP with extra features, such as:

  1. Zoom past the default zoom levels with a custom zoom by going to View > Zoom > Custom... and entering any zoom level. Useful for pixel art.
  2. Triangle tool. It is slightly glitchy, though.
  3. Toolbar for quick access to menu commands such as image properties, view grid and view thumbnail.
  4. Add shadow to your font.
  5. New and improved icons

More will come soon. Features accurate as of update v3.
